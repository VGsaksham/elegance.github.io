Our Story

Discover the passion and craftsmanship behind our brand, and the values that guide everything we create.

Our Beginning

Founded in 2010, Elegance began as a small workshop dedicated to creating handcrafted leather goods that would stand the test of time. Our founder, Elena Moretti, combined her Italian heritage and passion for traditional craftsmanship with modern, minimalist design principles.

What started as a small collection of leather handbags has grown into a comprehensive range of accessories that maintain the same dedication to quality and attention to detail that defined our very first creations.

Our Values

Craftsmanship

Every stitch, every cut, and every finish is executed with precision and care. We believe that true luxury lies in the details that others might overlook.

Sustainability

We source our materials responsibly and design our products to last for years, not seasons. Our commitment to sustainability guides every decision we make.

Timeless Design

We create pieces that transcend trends, focusing on clean lines, balanced proportions, and functional elegance that remains relevant year after year.
