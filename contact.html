Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary depending on the destination. Please contact us for specific information about your location.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase for items in their original condition. Custom orders are non-returnable. Please contact us to initiate a return.

Do you offer product customization?

Yes, we offer customization services for select products. Please contact us directly to discuss your specific requirements and for pricing information.

How do I care for my Elegance products?

Each product comes with specific care instructions. Generally, our leather goods should be kept away from excessive moisture and direct sunlight. We recommend regular conditioning with appropriate leather care products.
